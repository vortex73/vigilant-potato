We find ourselves in the midst of the surge of Artificial Intelligence related hoo-haa driven by the burgeoning corporate interests. Companies vying to oust each other in the race to deliver cutting edge performance and quality models to the end users.

Often someone a little or more detached from this can find the concept daunting to approach. ModelForge aims to solve precisely this by lowering the barrier to entry to creating cutting edge models in terms of technical skills required.

What is this blog?

This blog serves as a prolegomenon(cool word for intro) to what this project is about, the scope and delving into some technical details.

Prerequisites

  • Comfort with Python: Duh,ML,Duh. Though I prefer C++ for ML projects(prolly worth another blog lol).

    • Object Oriented Programming in Python: It helps a lot with code readability and organisation.
    • Working with modules, virtualenvs, pip and that entire lore.

  • Intuition of ML concepts: you ultimately build an extension of what you know. So a solid grasp on concepts you wish to implement.
